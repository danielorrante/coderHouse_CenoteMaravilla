Frequently asked questions

Do you pick up from my hotel?

We pick up from any hotel in Cancun, Puerto Morelos, and Costa/Playa Mujeres. If you are staying on an Airbnb we set up a convenient meeting point.

What's the cancellation policy

Plans have changed? Reschedule or cancel with no cost, just let us know at least 12 hours in advance. Unfortunately, if the tour is canceled after that 12-hour mark, this will be considered a No-Show and no refund will be provided.

Do I need a PADI certification

Yes, with the proper cave certification. We also provide the certification. Contact us for more information.
